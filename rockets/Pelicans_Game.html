Pelicans Game

Written by Tanzim Hasan


Date: March 3, 2020
Opponent Played: New Orleans Pelicans
Final Gamescore: Pelicans 94 - Rockets - 114
Tags: #ZionWilliamson #smallBall #Russell Westbrook

Now this felt like a game. I personally think this was the best team play the Rockets have had all year, especially in their small ball syste. Harden let off the gas and let Westbrook run the floor and have himself a monstorous game, scoring 55 points, 12 rebounds, 13 assists, 3 steals and 4 blocks to lead him to his 140th triple double. His shot was excellent, shooting 76% from the field and 44% from 3. He managed to weave the Pelicans defense in the backcourt to open up the lanes to attack the rim far more often than usual, giving the Rockets a ridiculous momentum throughout the entire game.
To the credit of the Pelicans, they mamnaged to keep pace with the Rockets for awhile, even almost outscoring the Rockets in the 3rd quarter. Their young core in ZIon Williamson, Lonzo Ball, and Brandon Ingram looked especially menacing. Once they piece together more players and improve their chemistry, the Pelicans are going to be a serious threat.
